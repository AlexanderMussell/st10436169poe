Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I sign up for music lessons?
You can sign up by completing the enquiry form on this page. Once submitted, we’ll contact you within 24–48 hours to confirm your details and schedule your first lesson.
Q2: What instruments do you teach?
  • Theory in any instrument
  • Guitar (acoustic and electric)
  • Piano
Q3: Are lessons available online or only in person?
  • Online lessons from home
  • In-person lessons
Q4: What age groups do you teach?
We teach students of all ages including children, teenagers, and adults.
Q5: How long is each lesson?
Lessons typically range between 30 minutes to 1 hour depending on the student’s needs.
